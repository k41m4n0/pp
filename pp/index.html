About Me

Personal goals:

  • Combining the affinity with the use of technology and the passion for music to make a positive impact in the community, by promoting creativity and wellbeing.
  • Finding a way to channel the advantages of working with digital resources to make a tangible impact on society, by fighting inequalities.

At this point of my career, my goal is to leverage the confidence I developed in working in highly dynamic environments, to find a role where my passion for music is valuable. What drives me is the opportunity to learn and travel, as well as working creatively.
